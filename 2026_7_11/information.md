# Shahr & Jang (City & War)
This is a game that I have been playing around with for some time in love2d. Inspired by 50 YEARS by Sasha 
Golovkin, instead of focusing entirely on state decisions, this game shifts its focus into physically being 
in the fighting and events around the state. You control a player and every day you have to make a decision 
as to what you want to allocate the resources of your people for. You can choose to explore, gather, or even 
change the location of your settlement.
